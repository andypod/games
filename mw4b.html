About

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 4 is a first-person shooter game developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. It is the fourth installment in the Modern Warfare series.

The game features a gripping single-player campaign, fast-paced multiplayer modes, and a cooperative mode for intense team-based gameplay.

Key Features

  • Intense and realistic combat
  • Immersive graphics and sound effects
  • Wide range of weapons and equipment
  • Deep customization options
  • Engaging multiplayer modes
  • Challenging cooperative missions
